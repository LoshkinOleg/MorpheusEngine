# Session 1 — The Silence at Pell's Draw
Date (in-fiction): Day 1–2 of the contract (exact calendar not established)
Location at end: Open hardpan, approximately 2 hours northeast of Pell's Draw

## Recap
- Captain Gus of the mercenary crawler *Iron Maiden* (crew of 20) was waiting outside Kettavar on a stalled escort contract arranged by broker Holt — a water convoy from Pell's Draw to Duskholm that never materialized.
- In Kettavar, Gus discovered Holt's office shuttered and abandoned 2-3 days prior, following a visit from an unknown man. A neighbor reported Holt left busy rather than frightened.
- Gus made contact with the *Ironback* (Captain Harven, crew of 12, out of Duskholm), who had been hired on the same contract from the Duskholm end. The *Ironback* carried fresh hull damage from a probing attack by unmarked sand bikes five days out of Duskholm.
- Both crawlers departed Kettavar in the evening, traveling through the night in coordinated formation toward Pell's Draw.
- At dawn, scouts located the *Ironback's* missing scout (Rennow) alive in an erosion channel — beaten, dehydrated, with a broken arm. He had found two stripped and abandoned water tankers southwest, and observed Pell's Draw standing but silent, gates open, no activity. He was run down by three bikes on his return but left alive deliberately.

## Lore Delta
* **Iron Maiden:** Small mercenary sandcrawler captained by Gus. Crew of 20. Armed with twin 76mm HE guns (port and starboard) and a 60mm mortar in static defensive positions. Carries two sand bikes and one lightly armed buggy. Heavier armor than the *Ironback*.
* **Ironback:** Small mercenary sandcrawler out of Duskholm, captained by Harven. Crew of 12, eight combat-rated. Armed with two forward heavy machine guns and one amidships swivel deck cannon. Carries one boarding truck and four sand bikes. Recently sustained hull scrape damage from an unmarked probing attack.
* **Holt:** A logistics broker operating out of Kettavar who arranged the Pell's Draw convoy escort contract. Hired both the *Iron Maiden* and *Ironback* for the same job without informing either. Abandoned his Kettavar office 2-3 days ago following a visit from an unknown individual. Whereabouts unknown.
* **Pell's Draw:** Small oasis town, population ~400-500. Economy based on facilitating transit trade between larger settlements. Modest defenses. Has been silent on all radio frequencies for at least four days. Gates observed open, no activity visible from a distance. Status unknown.

## Open Threads
- Pell's Draw is silent and apparently abandoned — cause unknown.
- Two of six convoy water tankers found stripped and abandoned southwest of current position.
- The three bikes that attacked Rennow left him alive deliberately — unknown significance.
- Holt's disappearance and his motive for hiring two crews without disclosure remain unexplained.
- The unmarked bikes that probed the *Ironback* east of Kettavar may be connected to the broader situation.
- Rennow found no bodies at the abandoned tankers — fate of convoy crew and town population unknown.
